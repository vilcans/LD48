Crown of the Mountain King

This game for the ZX Spectrum 48 was made in 48 hours for the 48th Ludum Dare competition. After the competition, it has been improved with instructions and a proper completion state. There is still room for improvement of course! Let me know what you think (fosstodon.org/@vilcans).

Play the game in the emulator above or download Z80 snapshot or TAP file to use on emulator or real hardware.
